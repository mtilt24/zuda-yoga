Step away from the noise of daily life and remember what it feels like to be fully alive.

Each day weaves together practices that awaken the body, restore balance, and deepen your connection to yourself, both on and off the mat. Located in the heart of Playa Guiones, Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort offers luxury accommodations surrounded by tropical beauty, minutes from the ocean.

Held with care.

Jimmy and Heidi bring years of teaching, warmth, and a deep love for the practice. They create the kind of space where students feel met, seen, and supported, on the mat and beyond it.

Jimmy Wheeler
Lead Teacher · RYT-500

Internationally certified master teacher based in Bozeman, Montana. Jimmy founded the Sacred Reset retreats and teaches in the Ashtanga and Seven Doorways traditions with creative, modern sequencing rooted in breath and presence. His classes are dynamic, accessible, and full of heart.

Heidi Hemler
Co-Teacher · MS, E-RYT 200, YACEP

Kinesiologist with over 30 years teaching movement and 20+ teacher trainings to her name. Heidi accepted the honor of ownership of Zuda Yoga and has guided hundreds of new teachers. Students love her energetic classes and nurturing spirit.
