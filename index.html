A return to yourself

Step away from the noise. Remember what it feels like to be fully alive.

Nestled in the lush jungle of Nosara, this retreat is an invitation to slow down, soften the nervous system, and reconnect to what matters most.

Through yoga, meditation, dharma, nourishing meals, and meaningful community, we create space to move with presence and rest with intention. Each day weaves together practices that awaken the body, restore balance, and deepen your connection to yourself, both on and off the mat.

Located in the heart of Playa Guiones, Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort offers luxury accommodations surrounded by tropical beauty, minutes from the ocean. Spend your afternoons surfing, exploring the beach, relaxing by the pool, booking a spa treatment, or simply embracing the rhythm of Costa Rica.
